FAQ

  • What is an NFT?

    NFT stands for "Non-Fungible Token". An NFT is a digital file whose unique proof of ownership is stored decentrally in a blockchain for all to see. Therefore, each NFT has a single owner and can only be resold by that owner. An NFT cannot be copied or exchanged for another file and is therefore unique.

  • What is special about a NFT?

    NFTs make it possible to identify a real owner for virtual goods. Thanks to the blockchain technology, forgeries cannot occur. Thus, virtual works of art become unique collector's items.

  • Which blockchain technology do you use?

    For the technology, we rely on the infrastructure of Polygon. The Polygon chain is a layer 2 Ethereum chain and provides greatly reduced gas fees. Polygon are actively committed to a sustainable Web3 and have just committed in their own "Green Manifesto" to achieve climate neutrality before the end of 2022. In contrast to the so-called proof-of-work, Polygon uses proof-of-stake validation, which means lower energy consumption.
